How to take a screenshot on ChromeOS

Screenshot Region

Some chromebooks have a dedicated Screenshot key.

1

Press the Screenshot key. If your chromebook does not have this key, press Shift + Control + Show Windows. Some keyboards do not have the Show Windows key, and you should substitute it with the F5 key.

2

Select a region of the screen by clicking and dragging.

3

You can choose to save or copy the screenshot in the notification that appears.
