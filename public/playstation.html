Playstation 5

The Playstation 5 allows you to take screenshots during gameplay.

1

Press the Capture button.

2

Select the Take Screenshot option to save the screenshot.

An icon will appear in the top right corner of the screen to indicate a screenshot has been taken.

3

You screenshot can be found in the Media Gallery app.

Extra: Shortcut

You can take a screenshot quickly by pressing and holding the Capture button until the screenshot icon appears in the top right.

Playstation 4

The Playstation 4 allows you to take screenshots during gameplay.

1

Press the Share button.

2

Press the Triangle button to save the screenshot.

3

You screenshot can be found in the Capture Gallery app.

Extra: USB

In the Capture Gallery app, you can transfer your screenshots to a USB storage device.
