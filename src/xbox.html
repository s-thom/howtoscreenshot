Full Screen

The Xbox One allows you to take screenshots during gameplay. You must have Xbox Live Gold to be able to capture screenshots.

1

Press the Xbox button.

2

Press the Y button to save the screenshot.

3

You screenshot can be found in the Upload app.

Extra: OneDrive

After taking the screenshot, you can Share it to different services, including OneDrive.

Your screenshot will appear in OneDrive\Pictures\Xbox Screenshots.
